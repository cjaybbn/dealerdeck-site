Capabilities

Built around how reps actually work.

Every feature answers a question reps ask on the floor ten times a day. No fluff, no dashboards to maintain, no data entry that isn't self-serving.

Voice search that understands you

Say "eggs 5 50 ease" and get every X5 xDrive50e on the lot. Gemini-powered query normalization handles accents, shorthand, and noise.

Know where every vehicle is

On lot, in transit, in service, sold pending. Status and physical location, tracked separately — the way actual dealerships operate.

Deals that log themselves

Log customers and progress with your voice. The app threads interactions, flags stakeholders, and surfaces what needs follow-up — so reps move on the right deal next.

Smart name resolution

"Dave" resolves to David. "Ed" to Edward. Disambiguation built in, so mentions of teammates always land on the right person — instantly.

Notifications only when relevant

Alerts route to deal-linked users, last updaters, and mentioned staff only. Reps see what affects them — never everything.

Safe to show on the floor

Built with dealer etiquette in mind. Everything reps see is safe to turn the phone around — no internal finance, no pricing games, no surprises.

How it works

Four stages. Zero friction.

DealerDeck is designed to earn trust before it asks for it. Every phase is opt-in and proves value before the next one begins.

STAGE 01

Deploy

Reps install from TestFlight, sign in, and have full inventory intelligence in under a minute. No IT, no integrations.

STAGE 02

Observe

Read-only keylog sync reads dealership inventory sheets and reflects them live. Zero changes to existing workflow.

STAGE 03

Integrate

Once adoption is proven, DealerDeck connects to the CRM — DriveCentric — for two-way deal sync and customer context.

STAGE 04

Scale

Location and inventory systems migrate to DealerDeck as the source of truth. Expansion to additional rooftops.
