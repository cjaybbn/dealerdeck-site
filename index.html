Capabilities

Built around how reps actually work.

Every feature answers a question reps ask on the floor ten times a day.

Voice search that understands you

Say "eggs 5 50 ease" and get every X5 xDrive50e on the lot. Gemini-powered query normalization handles accents, shorthand, and background noise.

Know where every vehicle is

On lot, in transit, in service, sold pending. Status and physical location are tracked separately — the way actual dealerships operate, not the way legacy software assumes.

Deals that log themselves

Log customers and progress with your voice. The app threads interactions, generates summaries, and surfaces next steps — so reps always know what to move on next.

Name resolution that just works

"Dave" resolves to David. "Ed" to Edward. Disambiguation is built in, so mentions of teammates always land on the right person instantly.

Notifications only when relevant

Alerts route only to deal-linked users, last updaters, and mentioned staff. Reps see what affects them — never everything.

Safe to turn the phone around

Every screen is safe to show a customer. No F&I spoilers. No private notes exposed. Basic estimates only — F&I stays with F&I.

How it works

Four stages. Earn trust first.

Each stage delivers value before we ask for more access.

STAGE 01

Inventory intelligence

Active today. Reps install from TestFlight, sign in, and get full inventory search, vehicle tracking, and customer logging in under a minute. Zero integration required.

STAGE 02

Keylog sync

In progress. Read-only mirror of the dealership's Google Sheets keylog, polling every two minutes. Write-back follows once accuracy is proven.

STAGE 03

DriveCentric CRM

Planned. Two-way sync with DriveCentric for customer data, deal notes, and AI summaries. Pending Partner Program approval.

STAGE 04

Source of truth

Planned. Location and inventory migrate to DealerDeck as the source of truth. Expansion to additional rooftops.
